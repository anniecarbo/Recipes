Spices and Pumpkin Muffins

This recipe is originaly from Leslie Chestermann

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup pecans
  • 1 ½ cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup candied ginger
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Prepare your muffin pan by lining it with 12 muffin cups
  3. Roast the pecans for about 7 minutes. Let them cool and roughly cut them into pieces.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and spices. Set aside.
  5. In a separate large bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Whisk in the eggs, one at the time and add candied ginger.
  6. Incorporate dry ingredients with wet ingredient. Whisk in the pumkin puree.
  7. Delicatly fold in pecans.
  8. Pour batter into the muffin linings. Sprinkle on top the sugar and additionnal cinnamon.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
